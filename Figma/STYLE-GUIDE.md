## Typography

### Font families

- **Headings (display):** Rajdhani — Weights: 500, 600, 700. Google Fonts.
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans — Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700. Google Fonts.

### Type scale

| Style | Font | Size | Line height | Letter spacing | Use |
|-------|------|------|-------------|----------------|-----|
| **Display** | Rajdhani | 36px | 40px | -0.02em | Section titles (e.g. "Our mission", "What we do") |
| **Title** | Rajdhani / Plus Jakarta | 24px | 28px | -0.01em | Card titles, H3 |
| **Body** | Plus Jakarta Sans | 15px | 24px | 0 | Paragraphs |
| **Caption** | Plus Jakarta Sans | 13px | 20px | 0 | Labels, meta, small copy |

### Hero headline

- **Font:** Rajdhani Bold  
- **Size:** 48px (desktop), 36px (tablet), 30px (mobile)  
- **Line height:** 1.1–1.2  
- **Color:** `#f8fafc`

### Eyebrow / overline

- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans Medium  
- **Size:** 13px  
- **All caps, letter-spacing:** 0.1em  
- **Color:** `#facc15` (accent)

---

## Spacing

- **Base unit:** 8px  
- **Section padding (vertical):** 80px (mobile), 96px (desktop)  
- **Section padding (horizontal):** 16px (mobile), 24px (desktop)  
- **Max content width:** 1152px (centered)  
- **Gap between cards / items:** 24px  
- **Gap between elements in a block:** 8px, 12px, 16px (1–2 units)

---

## Border radius

- **Cards, panels:** 12px  
- **Buttons:** 8px  
- **Inputs:** 8px  
- **Small pills / tags:** 6px

---

## Shadows

- **Card default:** `0 1px 2px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`, `0 4px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.12)`  
- **Card hover:** `0 8px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.18)`, `0 0 0 1px rgba(250,204,21,0.25)`  
- **CTA glow (primary button):** `0 0 20px rgba(250,204,21,0.35)`

---

## Gradients (for dividers / overlays)

- **Ukraine flag bar:** Linear left → right: transparent → `#0057b7` → `#ffdd00`  
- **Section divider (mission):** Linear: `#512da8` (60% opacity) → accent (50%) → `#ffdd00` (60%)  
- **Hero overlay:** Soft purple from top-left: `#512da8` at ~15% opacity fading to transparent

---

## Icons & imagery

- **Ukraine flag:** Two horizontal stripes, blue `#0057b7` (top), yellow `#ffdd00` (bottom). Use for favicon and header logo.
- **Wheat motif:** Optional low-opacity pattern or icon; accent color `#facc15` at 6–12% opacity for texture.
- **Placeholder areas:** Use text such as "Slider or video goes here", "Photo, video or slider goes here", "Add photo" with `#7887af` (text subtle).
