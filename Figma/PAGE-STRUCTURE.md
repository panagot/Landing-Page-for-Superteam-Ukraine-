## Mobile adaptation notes

- **Header:** Hamburger menu; nav in drawer.
- **Sections:** Single column; cards stack; same order.
- **Hero:** Headline 30px; buttons full width or stacked.
- **What we do:** 3 cards → 1 column.
- **By the numbers:** 4 cards → 2×2 or 1 column.
- **Events:** Horizontal scroll for cards or stacked.
- **Partners:** Cards wrap or stack.
- **Touch targets:** Min 44px height for buttons and links.
- **Padding:** 16px horizontal; section vertical 80px (or 20/24 in 8px grid).
